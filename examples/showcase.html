Content Slide

The most common slide type — text blocks, bullet lists, and numbered lists

  • Content slides support mixed block types in any order
  • Bullet lists use accent-colored markers for visual hierarchy
  • Numbered lists show circular step indicators
  • KPI cards auto-detect from xlarge + small text pairs

Content slides are the workhorse — use them for explanations, feature lists, and detailed breakdowns.
